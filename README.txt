In order to move the camera we are going to use the WASD keys as well as the mouse:

 	-Move forward:  W
	-Move backward:	S
	-Move right: 	D
	-Move left: 	A

	-Look Up: 	Mouse Up
	-Look Down:	Mouse Down
	-Look Right:	Mouse Right
	-Look Left: 	Mouse Left

	-Reset Camera:	R

The move is limited up and down according to real life head movement
